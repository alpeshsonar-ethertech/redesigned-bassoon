✅ Did it work? (next-day check)

Drop in your own recent enforcement records and ChokePoint forecasts the next day's choke points with the trained model — the same engine the rest of the dashboard uses. Optionally add the real next-day file to score the forecast.

1 · Recent history required
A CSV of recent events. Use at least 2 weeks (7 days minimum) so the model has enough history for its lag & rolling features. We forecast the day after your latest date.
2 · Actual next day optional
The real records for the predicted day. Add it to measure accuracy (hit-rate & concentration). Leave empty to just forecast.
Expected columns: created_datetime, latitude, longitude, police_station, vehicle_type, offence_code (location recommended). Files are processed locally by your server — see INTEGRATION.md.
